Rain sensor unable to control wipers.

Affected vehicles

  • All models with rain sensor.

Problem

  • Rain sensor unable to control wipers.

Cause

    Cause

  • Rain sensor function inoperative due to rain sensor mounting plate detached from windscreen.

Remedy

Parts

Adhesive1 633 7801
  • Remove rain sensor from mounting plate.
  • Thoroughly clean rain sensor mounting plate.
  • Thoroughly clean windscreen.
  • Apply adhesive to area indicated Fig.1173343.1.
  • Fit rain sensor mounting plate to windscreen.
  • Wait at least 5 hours for adhesive to dry before refitting rain sensor.
